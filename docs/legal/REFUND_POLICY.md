# Refund Policy

**Zyrcon, Inc.**
Effective Date: January 1, 2026

---

## 1. Overview

Zyrcon, Inc. wants you to be satisfied with your purchase. This Refund Policy describes when and how refunds are issued for Cascadia OS subscriptions and DEPOT operator purchases. All refunds are processed through Stripe and credited to your original payment method.

---

## 2. Eligibility Window

Refund requests must be submitted within **seven (7) calendar days** of the original purchase date. Requests submitted after this window will not be eligible for a standard refund, except as described in Section 7 (Exceptions).

This policy applies to:

- **Pro and Enterprise subscriptions** (monthly and annual)
- **Paid DEPOT operators** purchased individually or as part of a bundle

---

## 3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

1. Email **legal@zyrcon.ai** with the subject line: `Refund Request — [Your Account Email]`
2. Include in the body: your account email address, the name of the subscription or operator, the purchase date, and the reason for your request
3. Zyrcon will acknowledge your request within two (2) business days and notify you of the outcome within five (5) business days

Approved refunds are processed through Stripe and typically appear on your statement within **5–10 business days**, depending on your bank or card issuer.

---

## 4. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

- The **Lite (free) tier** — no charges apply, so no refund is applicable
- **Usage already consumed** — if an operator has been used to process a significant volume of tasks, Zyrcon may deny or reduce the refund at its discretion
- **Operator purchases older than seven (7) days** — outside the standard refund window
- **Custom Enterprise agreements** — refund terms for Enterprise contracts are negotiated separately and govern over this policy

---

## 5. Partial Refunds for Annual Plans

If you subscribed to an annual Pro or Enterprise plan and request a refund after the 7-day window but within the first 30 days, Zyrcon may, at its discretion, issue a **pro-rated refund** for the unused portion of the subscription year. Pro-rated refunds are calculated as:

> Refund Amount = (Annual Fee / 365) × Days Remaining

Pro-rated refunds are not available after 30 days from the start of the annual billing period. Monthly subscription fees are not pro-rated.

---

## 6. DEPOT Operator Refunds

Refunds for paid DEPOT operators are subject to the same 7-day window. Because operators run locally, Zyrcon cannot verify operator functionality on your specific system configuration; we encourage you to review the operator description, requirements, and user reviews before purchasing.

If an operator fails to install or launch at all, Zyrcon will issue a full refund regardless of the time elapsed since purchase, provided you submit documentation (error logs or screenshots) demonstrating the installation failure.

---

## 7. Exceptions

Zyrcon will issue refunds outside the standard policy in the following circumstances:

- **Fraud or unauthorized charges:** If you believe a charge was made without your authorization, contact legal@zyrcon.ai immediately. Zyrcon will investigate and, if the charge is confirmed unauthorized, issue a full refund and cooperate with any applicable dispute process through Stripe.
- **Uninstall within 24 hours:** If you uninstall a paid operator within 24 hours of purchase and have not executed any workflows with it, you are automatically eligible for a full refund upon request. No additional justification is required.
- **Platform-side defect:** If a confirmed bug or defect in Cascadia OS or DEPOT directly caused the product to be unusable, Zyrcon will issue a full refund at its discretion, regardless of time elapsed.

---

## 8. Disputes and Chargebacks

We encourage you to contact us before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer. Chargebacks initiated without first contacting Zyrcon may result in account suspension. Zyrcon will work in good faith to resolve billing disputes directly.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

Zyrcon may update this Refund Policy at any time. The policy in effect at the time of your purchase governs that transaction. Updated policies will be posted at `https://zyrcon.ai/legal/refunds` and communicated via email for material changes.
