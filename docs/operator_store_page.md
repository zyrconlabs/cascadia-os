## How DEPOT Works

**1. Browse**
Find operators by category, integration, or use case. Every listing includes capabilities, required permissions, and a compatibility badge for your Cascadia OS tier.

**2. Install**
Click Install. Cascadia OS downloads the operator, validates the manifest, and starts it automatically. No configuration files to edit.

**3. Activate**
Grant the permissions the operator requests. Operators run locally — your data never leaves your machine.

**4. Use**
The operator appears in your PRISM dashboard and chat interface immediately. Capabilities integrate with existing workflows.

## For Operator Developers

Build and publish operators for the Cascadia OS ecosystem.

**Requirements:**
- `manifest.json` with declared capabilities and permissions
- HTTP service with `/api/health` endpoint
- Compliance with Cascadia OS operator security standards

**Publish to DEPOT:**
Submit your operator via the developer portal at [zyrcon.store/developers](https://zyrcon.store/developers). Operators go through a security review before listing.

**Revenue share:** 70% to developer, 30% platform fee.
