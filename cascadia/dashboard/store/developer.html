How it works

1

Build your operator

Use the Cascadia OS operator template. Your operator needs: operator.py, manifest.json, install.sh, uninstall.sh, health.py, and tests. It runs locally on the user's machine — you never receive their data.

2

Submit for review

Fill in the form below with your name, email, and GitHub repository. Our team reviews within 5 business days. We check for quality, security, and manifest compliance.

3

Publish and earn

Once approved, your operator appears in DEPOT. Users can one-click install it. You receive monthly payouts via Stripe for all purchases and subscriptions.

Quality requirements

📋 Every operator must have

  • Valid manifest.json (schema v1)
  • install.sh and uninstall.sh
  • health.py endpoint
  • Test suite with ≥80% pass rate
  • README.md with usage docs
  • Approval gate for external actions

🚫 Not permitted

  • Sending user data to external servers
  • Requiring cloud accounts without disclosure
  • Modifying other operators' files
  • Accessing system credentials directly
  • Bypassing the PRISM approval flow
  • Malicious code of any kind

✅ Content standards

  • Business purpose clearly stated
  • No spam or bulk-message automation
  • No surveillance or tracking operators
  • Compliant with GDPR and CCPA

💰 Pricing rules

  • Free operators: always free
  • Paid operators: monthly subscription
  • Payments via Stripe (we handle billing)
  • Refunds: 7-day policy applies
